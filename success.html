Measuring Success

Success will be evaluated through two main categories: Key Success Metrics and Continuous Improvement.

Key Success Metrics

  • Defect Rate Reduction: Fewer than two high-severity defects post-deployment.
  • Timely Delivery: Adherence to set project timelines, especially for critical testing phases.
  • Stakeholder and Customer Satisfaction: Positive feedback through post-deployment surveys and meetings.
  • Standards Compliance: Full compliance with internal policies and industry standards.

Continuous Improvement

  • Analyse defect trends to uncover recurring issues.
  • Update testing frameworks and strategies based on lessons learned.
  • Document insights to guide future system changes and optimisations.
